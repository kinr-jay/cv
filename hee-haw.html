Hee-Haw Backpacking Trips

Yosemite National Park, April 2015

  • Founding of the Hee-Haw Wilderness Expedition Team
  • Five days, four nights
  • Entry and Exit TH: Happy Isles
  • Route: Through Little Yosemite Valley, up the Merced River Valley, over Red Peak Pass, back down the Illouette River Valley
  • Unplanned bivuoac at about 10,500' on the north side of Red Peak Pass in a snowstorm
Zion National Park, April 2016
  • Second Annual Hee-Haw
  • Two separate two-nighters:
    • Kolob Canyon
    • Southeastern Desert
San Juan National Forest, July 2017
  • Third Annual Hee-Haw
  • Two separate two-nighters:
    • West Fork of the San Juan River
    • Fourmile Lake
  • First experience with off-trail travel for several members
North Cascades National Park, June 2018
  • Fourth Annual Hee-Haw
  • While instrumental in organizing, Connor was unable to attend due to a broken collarbone
  • This region remains of high interest for further exploration
Big Bend National Park, April 2019
  • Fifth Annual Hee-Haw
  • Largest number of newcomers to the group
  • One one-nighter into the Chisos Mountains featuring a legendary lightning storm
  • Two nights at a primitive backcountry car camping site in the desert
The COVID-19 pandemic disrupted the annual big get-together, but plans are in the works to reinstate operations in 2021.
